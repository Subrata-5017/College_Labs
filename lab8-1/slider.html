Rhinoceros
Black Rhinoceros
It is one of five species of rhinoceros, and is native to Africa. Black rhinoceros are found in South Africa, Namibia, Zimbabwe, Botswana, and Kenya.Black rhinoceros are herbivores and eat a variety of plants, including grasses, leaves, and twigs. They are solitary animals and only come together to mate. Black rhinoceros are listed as critically endangered on the IUCN Red List of Threatened Species.

Leopard
Panthera pardus
It is a big cat known for its spotted coat, stealth, and strength. Leopards are solitary hunters and incredibly versatile, adapting to various habitats, from rainforests to savannas. They are excellent climbers and often carry their prey up into trees to avoid scavengers. The species is found across Africa and parts of Asia.
